Website Indoshoes

The Indoshoes website aims to promote and support the advancement of local shoe brands in Indonesia. Many users still struggle to discover and explore quality local shoe brands such as Aerostreet, Compass, Johnson, Athletika, and many others. Unfortunately, many people tend to prefer international brands like Adidas and others over local products. Therefore, we created this website to help enhance the visibility and popularity of local shoe brands in Indonesia, as well as to encourage people to better understand and appreciate domestic products.

UI/UX Website Indoshoes

The UI/UX design for the Indoshoes website aims to create an intuitive and engaging user experience while exploring local shoe brands in Indonesia. By understanding the challenges users face in finding quality local shoe brands, such as Aerostreet, Compass, Johnson, and Athletika, this design will facilitate a more enjoyable and seamless exploration.

We will integrate visually appealing elements and simple navigation to allow users to quickly find information about various products. Additionally, by emphasizing local brands, we aim to encourage the community to better recognize and appreciate domestic products. A responsive and aesthetically pleasing design will ensure that users feel connected to each brand and are more likely to choose local shoes over international brands like Adidas.
